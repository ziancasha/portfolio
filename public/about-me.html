Cinthya Castañeda

UX/UI Designer | FrondEnd | e-commerce | Digital Marketing

¡Welcome! It warms my heart that you are found in this segment. Not only because I will be discussing about myself, but also because I realize this will be our first interaction before we can actually talk face to face.

Well, I consider myself a 32-year-old person who has had quite an interesting life. The year 2018 was one of the most significant ones in my life because I have decided to shift my focus in my professional life. That is when I embarked on my journey in to the digital world. I left the security field and began working with the biggest marketplaces in Mexico, Mercado Libre and Amazon.

Two years later in 2020 I came across a web developer of e-commerce for the first time thus being able to learn how e-commerce works using its own platform. This discovery was revelationary in the sense that it shaped my career path towards the exciting field of Digital Marketing and Front-End Development.

In 2021, I had another great opportunity: to pursue a Diploma in UX/UI Design in Vancouver, Canada. I had learnt so much that was required in running an e-commerce business, and it was time to develop deeper understanding of my users and how they could be made happy. It was important for me to understand that great UX/UI design is one of the key principles of designing interfaces that users would love and appreciate. Thus, there was no way I was going to let this opportunity slip through my fingers.

Today, my calling is to help all those business who are still in conventional trade. I want to help them join an economy that is not only existing but will continue to develop into something much bigger.

Please be assured that I will look for a solution whenever there is an issue and no matter what I have to study or cultivate, I will do them so that I give you the best outcome.
